Ready to Join?

Getting started is easy

Step 1 - Get in Touch

Fill in our contact form or send us an email. We will get back to you with all the details you need.

Step 2 - Come Along

Come to a meeting and see what we are all about. No commitment needed - just turn up and join in.

Step 3 - Make Your Promise

Once you are ready, you will make your Scout Promise and officially become a member of 7th Gateshead.
