New Members - What to Expect

If you are coming for the first time, do not worry about not having a uniform yet. Just come along in comfortable clothes and join in.

A parent or guardian should stay for the first meeting so our leaders can meet them and answer any questions.

Any questions? Get in touch before coming along.
