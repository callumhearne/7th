What to Bring

For a typical weekly meeting

Uniform

  • Scout shirt (purple)
  • Scout scarf (necker)
  • Group woggle
  • Activity trousers or dark trousers

Essentials

  • Water bottle
  • Any signed forms or payments due
  • Pen and notebook
  • A good attitude!

For Outdoor Meetings

  • Waterproof jacket
  • Sturdy footwear
  • Warm layers in winter
  • We will let you know in advance

New Members - What to Expect

If you are coming for the first time, do not worry about not having a uniform yet. Just come along in comfortable clothes and join in.

A parent or guardian should stay for the first meeting so our leaders can meet them and answer any questions.

Any questions? Get in touch before coming along.
