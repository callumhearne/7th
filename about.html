About 7th Gateshead

Who we are, what we do, and what we stand for

Our Group

7th Gateshead Scout Group is part of Gateshead District and the wider Scout Association. We welcome young people aged 10.5 to 14 from Gateshead and the surrounding area. Our Scouts are supported by a dedicated team of adult volunteers who give their time to deliver an exciting, safe, and rewarding programme every week.

Scouting is about more than just badges and camps - it is about developing young people to have the skills, values, and confidence to make a positive difference in their communities and the world.

Our Values

The Scout Promise and Law guide everything we do

Integrity

We are honest, trustworthy, and keep our promises.

Respect

We respect ourselves, others, and the world around us.

Care

We support and look out for each other.

Belief

We explore what we believe and what matters to us.

Co-operation

We work as a team and help our community.

What We Get Up To

A packed programme of activities, adventures, and challenges

Camps & Expeditions

Weekend camps, summer camps, and expeditions are a highlight of the Scouting year. Sleeping under canvas, cooking on fires, and exploring new places together.

Badge Work

There are over 200 badges and awards available to Scouts. From cooking and photography to engineering and community impact, there is something for every interest.

Community Action

We get involved in local community projects, fundraising, and events. Scouts are encouraged to make a real difference in Gateshead.

Water Activities

Canoeing, kayaking, and sailing are just some of the water-based activities available to Scouts throughout the year.

Nights Away

From one-night sleepovers to week-long international trips, nights away build independence, resilience, and brilliant memories.

Weekly Meetings

Our regular weekly meetings cover games, challenges, skill-building, and planning - with something different every week.
